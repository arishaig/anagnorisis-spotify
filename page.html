Spotify

Import ratings from your Spotify library into Anagnorisis.

Setup required. Add the following to your config.yaml and restart: 
spotify_import:
  client_id: "YOUR_CLIENT_ID"
  client_secret: "YOUR_CLIENT_SECRET"
  redirect_uri: "https://YOUR_INSTANCE/spotify/callback"

Create a Spotify app at developer.spotify.com/dashboard and add the redirect URI to its allowlist.

Spotify import

Turn your Spotify listening history into ratings in your local library.

1 · Upload your data export

Request your data at spotify.com/account/privacy — tick Extended streaming history (play counts) and, for likes/dislikes/playlists, Account data. Spotify emails a .zip; upload it here (either export, or both).

2 · Preview, then import

Preview computes the ratings without changing anything. Review the spread, adjust the model in config.yaml if needed, then Import to write them.
